Stay Information

House Guide

Everything you need to know to make your stay comfortable and hassle-free.

Checking In & Out

  • Check-in time: After 3:00 PM
  • Self check-in with keypad (code: 2580)
  • Check-out time: Before 11:00 AM
  • Just close the door behind you when leaving
  • No need to clean, but please tidy up

The keypad is located to the right of the main door. Enter the 4-digit code followed by the # key. The door will unlock automatically. If you have any issues, please call me immediately.

For check-out, please make sure all windows are closed, lights are turned off, and the door is locked behind you. You can leave the keys on the kitchen counter.

House Rules

  • No smoking anywhere on the property
  • No parties or events
  • Quiet hours: 10 PM - 8 AM
  • No pets allowed
  • Maximum 4 guests at any time

We ask that you respect our neighbors by keeping noise levels reasonable, especially after 10 PM. The walls can be thin in places, and sound carries easily.

If you're traveling with children, please ensure they are supervised at all times, especially around the staircase and balcony areas.

A/C & Heating

  • Thermostat located in the hallway
  • Set between 68-78°F for comfort
  • A/C: Press "Cool" button
  • Heat: Press "Heat" button
  • Please turn off when leaving

The Nest thermostat can be adjusted by turning the dial clockwise to increase temperature or counterclockwise to decrease it. The display will show the current temperature and the target temperature.

For best efficiency, we recommend keeping the A/C around 75°F in summer and heat around 68°F in winter.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about your stay.

Is parking included with my stay?

Yes, one dedicated parking spot (#42) is included with your stay. You'll need the garage code (3791) to access the parking area. If you have additional vehicles, street parking is available but may require a permit or payment depending on the time of day.

How do I access the building?

To access the building, use the keypad at the main entrance and enter code 1234 followed by the # key. For your individual unit, use the keypad on the door with code 2580. If you have any issues, please call me immediately.

What is the best way to get around?

While having a car in LA is convenient, there are many options. Uber and Lyft are widely available. Public transportation includes Metro buses and trains, with a station just 3 blocks away. For shorter trips, electric scooters and bikes are available through apps like Bird and Lime.
